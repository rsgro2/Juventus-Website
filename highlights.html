Highlights

Being one of the top teams in Italy, Juventus tends to put on a show every game they play. In the past couple of years, the club has been able to score some outstanding world class goals. These goals have been scored in all sorts of high intensity matches: from Serie A championships to the Champion's League finals!

One Juventus goal in particular is Cristiano Ronaldo's famous high jump header. In a Serie A match against U.C. Sampdoria, Ronaldo scored a header to put Juventus at a winning position; 2 goals to 1. Ronaldo's goal made headlines as he jumped what is said to be almost 9 feet in the air. This will go down as one of Juventus FC's most iconic goals in recent history.
